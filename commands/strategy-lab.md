Your personal strategy lab. Bring any idea — from YouTube, a blog, a book, or your own intuition — and turn it into a testable hypothesis in under 5 minutes.

Read `skills/strategy-lab/SKILL.md` and follow the 4-step process:
1. Extract the rules from the user's description
2. Formalize into a Strategy Card (user approves before testing)
3. Backtest against real historical data
4. Compare head-to-head with other strategies

The lab saves every tested strategy to `profile/strategies/` for future reference.
