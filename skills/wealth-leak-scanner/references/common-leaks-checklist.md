# Common Wealth Leaks Checklist

## Tier 1: High Impact, Frequently Missed ($500+/year each)

1. **401k Match Not Maximized**
   - Average match: 50% of first 6% = 3% of salary
   - On $100K salary: $3,000/year FREE money left on table
   - Fix: Increase 401k contribution to at least match threshold
   - 20-year compound value of $3K/yr: $189,295

2. **RSU Cost Basis Error on Tax Return**
   - When RSUs vest, income is reported on W-2. When you sell, broker reports FULL sale proceeds on 1099-B often with $0 cost basis
   - IRS sees: sold $20K stock, cost basis $0 → $20K gain → $5,000 tax
   - Reality: vesting was already taxed as income, actual gain is only the appreciation since vest
   - Fix: Report correct cost basis (vest-date FMV × shares) on Form 8949
   - Typical overpayment: $1,000-10,000+ for tech workers

3. **Cash Earning 0.01% in Savings/Checking**
   - $20K in checking at 0.01% vs HYSA at 4.5% = $898/year lost
   - Fix: Move excess cash (beyond 1-month expenses) to HYSA
   - Options: Marcus (Goldman), Ally, Wealthfront Cash, SoFi

4. **High-Fee Mutual Funds (Expense Ratio >0.5%)**
   - $100K in fund with 1.0% ER vs 0.03% index: $970/year in fees
   - Over 30 years: ~$320K in lost wealth from fee drag alone
   - Fix: Replace with equivalent low-cost index fund (VOO, VTI, VXUS)

5. **ESPP Not Enrolled**
   - Typical ESPP: 15% discount on stock purchase
   - On $25K annual ESPP: $3,750 guaranteed profit (sell immediately for 15% return)
   - Even immediate-sell ESPP is one of the highest guaranteed returns available
   - Fix: Enroll at maximum contribution (typically 10-15% of salary)

6. **Mega Backdoor Roth Not Utilized**
   - If employer plan allows after-tax contributions + in-service distribution
   - Additional ~$36,500+/year into Roth
   - 20-year value at 10%: $2.3M in tax-free wealth
   - Fix: Check plan documents, set up after-tax contributions

## Tier 2: Medium Impact, Commonly Missed ($200-500/year each)

7. **Foreign Tax Credit Not Claimed**
   - International funds pay foreign taxes on dividends (shown on 1099-DIV Box 7)
   - Many filers miss claiming the credit on Form 1116 or direct credit
   - Typical value: $50-300/year for diversified international holdings
   - Fix: Claim on tax return; if total foreign tax <$300 single/$600 MFJ, can take direct credit without Form 1116

8. **HSA Not Maximized**
   - 2025 limits: $4,300 individual / $8,550 family
   - Triple tax benefit: deduction + tax-free growth + tax-free medical withdrawals
   - If invested (not spent on current medical), $4,300/yr at 10% for 20yr = $271,420
   - Fix: Enroll in HSA-eligible HDHP, max HSA, invest it, pay medical out-of-pocket

9. **Auto Insurance Not Shopped**
   - Average savings from switching: 20-30%
   - On $2,000/year policy: $400-600 savings
   - Fix: Get quotes from 3+ carriers every 2 years (Geico, Progressive, USAA, etc.)

10. **Dependent Care FSA Not Used**
    - Pre-tax $5,000/year for childcare expenses
    - At 24% bracket: $1,200/year tax savings
    - Fix: Enroll during open enrollment if you have childcare costs

11. **Student Loan Interest Deduction Not Taken**
    - Up to $2,500 deduction (above-the-line, don't need to itemize)
    - At 22% bracket: $550/year savings
    - Phase-out: $75K-90K single, $155K-185K MFJ
    - Fix: Ensure deduction is claimed on tax return

12. **Saver's Credit Not Claimed**
    - 10-50% credit on retirement contributions, up to $1,000/$2,000
    - Income limits: $38,250 single / $76,500 MFJ (2025)
    - Many eligible filers don't claim it
    - Fix: Ensure claimed on tax return if eligible

## Tier 3: Lower Impact but Easy Fixes ($50-200/year each)

13. **Bank Fees (Maintenance, ATM, Wire)**
    - Average American pays $7/month in bank fees = $84/year
    - Fix: Switch to no-fee bank (Schwab, Ally, SoFi, Fidelity CMA)

14. **Credit Card Not Optimized**
    - Not using a 2%+ cashback card for all spending
    - On $3,000/month spending: $720/year in missed rewards
    - Fix: Get a 2% flat cashback card (Citi Double Cash, Wells Fargo Active Cash)

15. **Commuter Benefits Not Used**
    - Pre-tax transit/parking up to $325/month
    - At 24% bracket on $200/month transit: $576/year savings
    - Fix: Enroll through employer

16. **Energy Credits Not Claimed**
    - Heat pump installation: 30% credit up to $2,000
    - Home insulation: 30% credit up to $1,200
    - Solar: 30% credit (no cap)
    - EV purchase: up to $7,500

17. **Library Card Not Replacing Subscriptions**
    - Libby/OverDrive: free ebooks, audiobooks (replaces Audible: $180/yr)
    - Kanopy: free movies (replaces some streaming)
    - Fix: Get library card, cancel redundant subscriptions

## Scan Methodology

When scanning, prioritize by: **Annual Value × Probability of Applying × Ease of Fix**

For each leak found, provide:
1. What it is (plain language)
2. How much it costs annually
3. Exactly how to fix it (step by step)
4. 20-year compound value of fixing it
5. Effort level: Easy (15 min) / Medium (1 hour) / Hard (needs professional)
