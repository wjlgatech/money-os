# Macro Signal Definitions & Thresholds

## VIX (CBOE Volatility Index)

Measures expected 30-day volatility of the S&P 500.

| Level | Status | Interpretation | Portfolio Action |
|-------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| < 15 | 🟢 Normal | Low fear, complacency | Maintain positions; consider building cash (markets may be overconfident) |
| 15-20 | 🟢 Normal | Healthy market anxiety | Standard operations |
| 20-25 | 🟡 Elevated | Increased uncertainty | Pause new purchases; review stop-loss levels |
| 25-30 | 🟡 High | Significant fear | Prepare buy list; stage cash for deployment |
| 30-40 | 🔴 Panic | Market selloff | Deploy tranche 1 (25% of cash reserve) into highest-conviction positions |
| > 40 | 🔴 Crisis | Extreme fear (2008, 2020 levels) | Deploy tranche 2-3; these levels historically mark generational buying opportunities |

**Where to check**: Search "VIX index today" or "CBOE VIX"

## US 10-Year Treasury Yield

The benchmark risk-free rate that anchors all asset valuations.

| Level | Status | Interpretation | Portfolio Action |
|-------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| < 3.0% | 🟢 Low | Accommodative; growth stocks favored | Lean into growth/tech |
| 3.0-4.0% | 🟢 Normal | Balanced environment | Standard allocation |
| 4.0-4.5% | 🟡 Elevated | Pressure on valuations | Trim speculative growth if over-allocated |
| 4.5-5.0% | 🟡 High | Significant headwind for equities | Reduce duration; favor cash-generative companies |
| > 5.0% | 🔴 Critical | Financial conditions tightening hard | Defensive rotation: reduce growth, add value/dividend stocks, increase cash |

**Where to check**: Search "US 10 year treasury yield today"

## Japan 10-Year Government Bond Yield (THE "BLACK SWAN" SIGNAL)

Japan holds ~$1.1 trillion in US Treasuries. Rising Japanese yields make domestic bonds more attractive, potentially triggering massive selling of US assets.

| Level | Status | Interpretation | Portfolio Action |
|-------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| < 0.5% | 🟢 Normal | BOJ yield curve control intact | No action |
| 0.5-1.0% | 🟡 Watch | BOJ loosening control | Monitor weekly; begin staging cash |
| 1.0-1.5% | 🟡 Elevated | Japanese investors may start repatriating | Increase cash reserve to 25%; reduce US Treasury-sensitive positions |
| 1.5-2.0% | 🔴 Warning | Capital repatriation likely underway | Increase cash to 30%; add gold exposure; reduce long-duration assets |
| > 2.0% | 🔴 Critical | Potential forced selling of US assets | Maximum cash position; prepare to buy the dip on quality assets |

**Where to check**: Search "Japan 10 year bond yield today"

**Mechanism**: When JGB yields rise → Japanese pension funds and insurance companies sell US Treasuries to buy JGBs → US Treasury yields spike → US equity valuations compress → selloff cascade.

## US Dollar Index (DXY)

Measures USD strength against basket of 6 major currencies.

| Level | Status | Interpretation | Portfolio Action |
|-------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| < 95 | 🟡 Weak | Dollar declining; benefits international holdings | Increase international exposure; gold tends to rise |
| 95-105 | 🟢 Normal | Standard range | No action |
| 105-110 | 🟡 Strong | Headwind for international holdings and commodities | Review international positions |
| > 110 | 🔴 Very Strong | Crushing international assets; EM debt stress | Reduce international exposure; watch for EM crises |

## Federal Funds Rate

| Direction | Status | Interpretation | Portfolio Action |
|-----------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| Cutting | 🟢 Tailwind | Risk-on; growth stocks benefit | Lean into growth positions |
| Paused | 🟢 Neutral | Waiting for data | Standard allocation |
| Hiking | 🟡 Headwind | Tightening; hurts valuations | Favor quality/cash flow over growth |
| Emergency cut | 🔴 Crisis | Something broke | Deploy cash aggressively into quality assets |
