# Surplus Cash Routing Decision Tree

## The Waterfall (in strict priority order)

### Tier 1: Guaranteed Returns (Do These First)

1. **401k Employer Match** — Priority: CRITICAL
   - If not capturing full match, this is the #1 priority regardless of anything else
   - Employer match = 50-100% instant return
   - Example: 50% match on 6% → contribute 6% minimum to get full match
   - If Roth 401k available AND user is in low bracket (<24%), prefer Roth 401k

2. **High-Interest Debt** (>7% APR) — Priority: CRITICAL
   - Credit cards (15-25% APR): pay off aggressively, guaranteed return at APR rate
   - Personal loans (>7%): accelerate payoff
   - Student loans: only if rate >7% AND no forgiveness program pending
   - Exception: if eligible for 0% balance transfer, use that + invest the difference

3. **Emergency Fund to 3 Months** — Priority: HIGH
   - Target: 3 months of essential expenses (not gross income)
   - Where to hold: HYSA (4%+ APR as of 2025), NOT checking account
   - If user has highly stable job + partner income, 2 months is acceptable minimum
   - If user has variable income or is sole earner, target 4-6 months

### Tier 2: Tax-Advantaged Growth (Maximum Compounding)

4. **Roth IRA** — Priority: HIGH
   - 2025 limit: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+)
   - Income limit: $150K single / $236K MFJ for direct contribution
   - If over income limit, use Backdoor Roth IRA
   - Why Roth before Traditional: tax-free growth + no RMDs + flexible withdrawals
   - Exception: if in 32%+ bracket NOW and expect lower in retirement, Traditional may win

5. **HSA** (if eligible) — Priority: HIGH
   - 2025 limit: $4,300 individual / $8,550 family
   - Triple tax advantage: deductible + tax-free growth + tax-free withdrawal for medical
   - Best strategy: invest HSA funds, pay medical out of pocket, let HSA compound
   - This is the most tax-advantaged account in existence

6. **Mega Backdoor Roth** (if employer plan allows) — Priority: HIGH
   - After-tax 401k contributions + in-plan Roth conversion
   - Additional ~$36,500+ into Roth per year (total 401k limit minus pre-tax contributions)
   - Check: does employer plan allow after-tax contributions AND in-service distributions?
   - If yes, this is one of the most powerful wealth-building tools available

7. **401k Max** — Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH
   - 2025 limit: $23,500 ($31,000 if 50+)
   - Pre-tax reduces current tax bill; Roth 401k grows tax-free
   - Decision: Pre-tax if in 32%+ bracket now; Roth if in 22% or lower

### Tier 3: Flexible Growth (After Tax-Advantaged Is Maxed)

8. **Taxable Brokerage** — Priority: MEDIUM
   - No contribution limits
   - Use for: broad index funds, tax-efficient ETFs, individual stock thesis
   - Tax-loss harvesting available here (hook to tax-harvest skill)
   - Prefer: long-term holds (LTCG rate) over frequent trading (ordinary income)
   - Asset location: put tax-inefficient assets in tax-advantaged; tax-efficient here

9. **529 Plan** (if kids) — Priority: MEDIUM
   - State tax deduction in many states
   - Tax-free growth for education
   - 2025: can roll unused 529 to Roth IRA (up to $35K lifetime, with conditions)
   - Don't over-fund: estimate actual education costs first

10. **Emergency Fund to 6 Months** — Priority: LOW-MEDIUM
    - After all the above, build to full 6-month cushion
    - Consider: T-bills or short-term treasury ETF for higher yield than HYSA
    - If self-employed, target 9-12 months

## Special Situations

### Variable Income (Freelancer/Commission)
- Route minimum 20% of each payment to tax escrow (estimated quarterly taxes)
- Build 6-month emergency fund before aggressive investing
- Use "pay yourself first" on every deposit: 50% essentials, 20% tax escrow, 20% invest, 10% discretionary

### Dual Income Household
- Prioritize maxing BOTH spouses' tax-advantaged accounts
- If one spouse has Mega Backdoor Roth access, maximize it
- Consider: one income covers expenses, other income 100% to investing

### Windfall (Bonus, Inheritance, Stock Vest)
- Do NOT invest lump sum all at once (emotional risk)
- Split: 1/3 immediate, 1/3 over 3 months, 1/3 over 6 months
- Exception: if clear tax-loss harvesting opportunity exists, deploy immediately to offset
- Always check tax bracket impact before deploying (hook to tax-strategy skill)
